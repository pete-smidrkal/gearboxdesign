T&T APP

THE APP

Training and Therapies is a platform that connects professionals in the wellbeing sector with clients. Users can book different wellbeing services in just a few clicks. Services are delivered to clients homes - or any other preferred location - to provide maximum convenience.

DEVELOPMENT

The app aesthetics was intended to be minimalist, clean and clear in order to make users  feel at ease and confident going through all stages of the booking process. No design elements like shapes or textures were to be present.

MOOD BOARD

Clear, Fresh and Natural: The inspiration was fresh water and food, and outdoor living. This mood board wants to demonstrate that healthy people is empowered to achieve anything they want because they are not held back by an unhealthy body or mind.

THE COLORS

LOGO

In designing the logo the intention was to immediately demonstrate health and wellbeing while maintaining simplicity. Browsing through hundreds of images which represented health and wellbeing the image of a healthy looking heart, inspired by the logo of the British Heart Foundation, became the idea to develop and it took form after a few sketches.

TYPOGRAPHY

A single font for the entire app was favoured in order to keep a simple style. San-serif Montserrat was elected for it's slick and clear aesthetics. Only light weights are used in both headlines and copy keeping differences subtle.

IMAGERY

To add a bit of human warmth and colour, imagery of healthy happy people is used. In order to make all of the photographs look and feel consistent with one another a filter was applied on top of them. However, they were kept light, perfectly colored, and not overly saturated.

EARLY WIREFRAMES

MVPs

1. User Account : An straight forward, quick and simple sign up process makes users not to be put off by it.

2. Booking system : Done quickly and easily in only a few steps.

3. Online payment : Online payment options at the click of a button, make the process easy and secure for all users.

4. Rating professionals : Providing users the option to rate the service received ensures maximum quality of service from each professional. 

5. Account management : From the menu users are able to:

- See their previous bookings and manage their upcoming ones. This means, cancelling or rescheduling a booking.

- See and make use of any promotional offer the company has available, without getting their emails spammed. 

- Manage their accounts. This means, change password and profile picture, edit home address and payment method, log out and close their account.

- Contact T&T if in need of any assistance.
